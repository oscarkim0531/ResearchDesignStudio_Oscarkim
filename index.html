"The Deprivation of Readability"

This project investigates how subscription-based economies strategically employ 'Visual Barriers' to obstruct user autonomy. While the 'Join' process is paved with intuitive UX, the 'Cancellation' path is intentionally cluttered with microscopic fonts, low contrast, and infinite scrolls—a digital fortress designed to exhaust the user’s cognitive will. This archive quantifies these dark patterns as a form of 'Time Extortion,' visualizing the labor forced upon us by the deliberate architecture of unreadability. It is a record of visual resistance against the systems that profit from our confusion.
